About

Who builds WithoutMe and why it exists.

MC Tools

MC Tools is a solo software studio run by Michael Cardin. We build focused, practical tools for small businesses — the kind of software that solves one problem well, without the complexity or price tag of enterprise platforms.

Every product under MC Tools is built with the same philosophy: let people start using it immediately, keep it simple, and charge a fair price.

Why WithoutMe exists

If you run a local service business, you already know the problem. The process for how things get done lives in your head. When you're not around, things get missed, done wrong, or not done at all. Your business can't run without you.

WithoutMe was built to fix that. It gives you a simple way to write down how your business operates — step by step — and hand it to your team. No training platform, no complicated workflow software. Just clear instructions anyone can follow.

Who it's for

Owners of local service businesses with 10–30 employees. HVAC, plumbing, cleaning, landscaping, electrical, pest control — the trades where consistent execution matters and the owner is usually the bottleneck.

How it's built

WithoutMe is a web application. It works in your browser on any device — no app download needed. Your SOPs are stored locally on your device so the tool works even without internet. If you create an account, your data syncs to the cloud so you can access it from anywhere.

Payments are handled by Paddle, which acts as our merchant of record. Your credit card information never touches our servers.
