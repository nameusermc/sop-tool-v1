What's included in Free?

Create unlimited SOPs with step-by-step instructions, use any of our pre-built templates (including 18 industry-specific templates for trades like HVAC, plumbing, cleaning, and more), and export any SOP as a PDF. Your data is stored on your device and works offline — no account required. Free has no time limit and no feature restrictions beyond AI, team sharing, and cloud sync.

What does Pro add?

AI-powered SOP creation: Add a title and description, and AI generates 5–8 detailed steps tailored to your business type. No blank-page problem — just real, industry-specific procedures in seconds.

AI clarity rewriting: Already have steps? AI rewrites them so a first-day employee could follow along with zero help. Every step becomes clear, specific, and actionable.

Team access: Create invite links and share them with your team. Team members click the link and see your SOPs immediately — no account required for them. They can run SOPs as checklists to complete tasks step by step.

Completion tracking: See exactly who completed which checklist, when they finished, and how long it took. Drill into step-level detail including tech notes your team leaves along the way. Filter by date range or employee — no more asking "did you do it?"

Daily digest email: Every morning you get an email summarizing yesterday's team activity — completions grouped by employee with SOP titles and timestamps. Know your crew is following the process without having to check. On by default, opt out anytime in account settings.

Cloud sync: Access your SOPs from any device. Create an account, sign in from your phone, laptop, or office computer, and your SOPs are always up to date.

Unlimited team members: No per-seat charges. Share with 5 people or 50 — the price is the same.

How billing works

Pro is billed monthly at $39/mo through Paddle, our secure payment processor. You can cancel anytime — your access continues through the end of the billing period, then reverts to Free. We offer a 14-day refund policy on your first payment. See our Refund Policy for details.

Who is this for?

WithoutMe is built for owners of local service businesses with 10–30 employees — HVAC, plumbing, cleaning, landscaping, electrical, pest control, and similar trades. If your team does the same tasks every day and you're tired of being the one who explains how, this tool is for you.
