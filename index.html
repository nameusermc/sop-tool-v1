What is WithoutMe?

WithoutMe is a web-based SOP (Standard Operating Procedure) tool for local service businesses with 10–30 employees. Create step-by-step procedures, share them with your team via a link, and let them run each task as a checklist. No training platform, no workflow automation — just clear steps your team follows without you.

Key Features

Unlimited SOP creation, 10 pre-built templates, PDF export, offline-first (works without internet), link-based team access (no signup required for team members), cloud sync across devices, and step-by-step checklist execution.

Pricing

Free — Unlimited SOPs, all templates, PDF export, offline use. Single device, no team sharing.

Pro — $39/mo — Everything in Free plus team access via shareable links, cloud sync across devices, and unlimited team members. Cancel anytime.

Payments processed by Paddle (Merchant of Record). 14-day refund policy on first payment.

Who is it for?

Owners of local service businesses — HVAC, plumbing, cleaning, landscaping, electrical, pest control. If your team does the same tasks every day and the process lives in your head, WithoutMe gets it out of your head and into their hands.
