WithoutMe

Stop being the bottleneck. Turn how you do work into clear steps your team can follow — without you.

What is WithoutMe?

WithoutMe is a web-based SOP (Standard Operating Procedure) tool for local service businesses with 10–30 employees. Create step-by-step procedures, share them with your team via a link, and let them run each task as a checklist. No training platform, no workflow automation — just clear steps your team follows without you.

Key Features

Unlimited SOP creation, 18 pre-built templates, PDF export, offline-first (works without internet), link-based team access (no signup required for team members), task assignments with due dates, per-employee completion tracking with daily digest email, images in steps, cloud sync across devices, and step-by-step checklist execution.

Pricing

Free — Unlimited SOPs, all templates, PDF export, offline use. Single device, no team sharing.

Pro — $39/mo — Everything in Free plus AI-powered steps, team access via shareable links, task assignments with due dates, completion tracking (see who finished what and when), daily digest email, images in steps, cloud sync across devices, and unlimited team members. Cancel anytime.

Payments processed by Paddle (Merchant of Record). 14-day refund policy on first payment.

Who is it for?

Owners of local service businesses — HVAC, plumbing, cleaning, landscaping, electrical, pest control. If your team does the same tasks every day and the process lives in your head, WithoutMe gets it out of your head and into their hands.
