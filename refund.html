Refund Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

Our Approach

WithoutMe offers a fully functional free plan so you can evaluate the product before paying anything. The free plan includes unlimited SOP creation, all templates, and PDF export — no credit card required, no time limit.

Because of this, we expect that most users will have a clear understanding of the Service before upgrading to Pro. That said, we want every customer to feel confident in their purchase.

Refund Eligibility

If you are unsatisfied with your Pro subscription, you may request a full refund within 14 days of your initial purchase. Refund requests after the 14-day window will be considered on a case-by-case basis.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact us at support@withoutme.app with your account email address. We aim to process all refund requests within 5 business days.

Refund Processing

Refunds are processed through Paddle (Paddle.com Market Limited), our payment processor and Merchant of Record. Once approved, the refund will be issued to your original payment method. Processing times depend on your bank or payment provider, typically 5–10 business days.

Cancellation vs. Refund

Cancelling your subscription is different from requesting a refund:

  • Cancellation — stops future charges. You keep Pro access until the end of your current billing period, then revert to the free plan.
  • Refund — returns payment for the current billing period. Pro access is reverted to the free plan immediately upon refund.

You can cancel your subscription at any time through your Paddle account management page. No refund is needed to cancel — you simply won't be charged again.

After Cancellation or Refund

Your SOPs are never deleted when you cancel or receive a refund. All SOPs remain accessible on the free plan. The only change is that team sharing and cloud sync features are deactivated. Any SOPs stored locally on your device remain untouched.

Questions about refunds? Contact us at support@withoutme.app.
